The story

Built one
tool at a time.

No funding, no team, no roadmap deck. Just 18 months of shipping small things that compound.

Jan2023

The password generator nobody asked for.

I built a password generator in a weekend because every existing one was either ugly, slow, or wanted my email. It got 41 visitors in week one. I almost shelved it.

Apr2023

Tool #4 — and the first paid month.

AdSense paid out $47. I bought a domain that wasn't a misspelling and started taking the project seriously. The JSON formatter went viral in a small Slack workspace.

Aug2023

The complete rewrite.

Threw out the original design, built a real token system, committed to the serif italic. Every old tool got rebuilt in two weeks. Bounce rate fell off a cliff.

Feb2024

200K monthly · quit day job.

Hit a number that covered my rent, my server bill, and groceries. Wrote a "goodbye" Slack message at the agency and never looked back. The first month of full-time was terrifying.

Now2024

48 tools · 2.1M monthly · still solo.

Shipping a new tool roughly every Friday. Currently obsessed with making the next version feel like a desktop app you'd pay for, except it's a web page that loads in 200ms.
